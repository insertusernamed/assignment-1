Hello, my name is Daniel and I am a junior developer. I have close to a decade of experience in multiple coding languages and have maintained a big part of the codebase at my workplace. My favourite genre of application is functional programming.

For the past year, I have been working on a real-time system to manage fire extinguishers in a building. Unlike other applications, the system is a library that an application can use or provide some services in, without creating a new independent program. When the time comes to develop must test their functionality over many years, it will be an enormous challenge. I thought it would be fun to focus on it in the challenge and see how the process of real-time software development and test changes.
